1.3 Gen-art

During the third period, you'll discover your own process as an artist, only one catch: your work must be randomly generated and have random input. After having experimented and failing a lot, I finally made my own generative landscape, which you can influence by using an Arduino. You can check the video about it here. After that I also made it into an app for the pc, without the use of an Arduino.

Learned

  • Processing (java)
  • Unreal Engine
  • Arduino
  • Video and audio design
  • Premiere Pro
  • Design research
